Impact of technology on the watch industry
and customer trends

The watch industry has dominated the market in today's generation. Espacially the digital and smartwatch. In past generation people wear tradional mechanical watch in that time there is no digital and smartwatch so the watch industry has only mechanical watch but in today's generation people are wearing digital and smatchwatch so the supply of mechanical watch is decreasing day by day. The customer has a trend to wear digital and smartwatch because of technology. Technology has made this world advanced which also affects in watch design and sales so because of that smartwatch and digital watch is selling more and more in this time.


Smartwatches has been emerged day by day. It has been the most valuable thing in watch industry. Everyone is using smartwatch in today's world. Companies like Samsung, Apple, Garmin etc have developed system like fitness training tracker, heartrate monitor, navigator, bloodpressure checker etc in smartwatch . Watch industry are using technology in order to sell smartwatch. They are using online advertisement to promote their product. They also announce new feature to promote and sell their products.
